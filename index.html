About Me

My introduction

Beatrice Njoki Nyamu is a licensed Psychological Counselor with over a decade of experience in mental health care and emotional support. Specializing in individual and group therapy, crisis intervention and adolescent counseling. With qualifications in both technical education and psychological counseling, having a unique ability to blend empathy with structured guidance. She is accredited by the Ministry of Health and the Kenya Counseling and Psychological Association and committed to helping clients navigate emotional challenges and achieve mental well-being.
